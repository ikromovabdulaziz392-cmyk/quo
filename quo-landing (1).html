FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We have answers.

How long does a transfer take?
+

Most transfers are completed within seconds to a few minutes. International transfers typically arrive within 1–2 business days depending on the destination country and payment method used.

What are the transfer fees?
+

Quo charges a flat fee of just 0.5% per transaction with a minimum of $0.50. Free plan users get 5 free transfers per month. Pro and Business users enjoy reduced fees on high-volume transfers.

Is my money safe with Quo?
+

Absolutely. Quo uses bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. Your funds are held in segregated accounts at licensed financial institutions.

Which countries does Quo support?
+

Quo currently supports transfers to and from 50+ countries across North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Middle East. We're constantly expanding our network to serve more regions.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period with no cancellation fees.
